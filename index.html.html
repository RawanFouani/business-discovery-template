Copper Cortex Discovery Checklist (Quick Reference)

Before proposing any software solution, confirm that you can confidently answer YES to each question:
⬜ Do we understand exactly how the business makes money?
⬜ Have we mapped the complete end-to-end workflow?
⬜ Do we know every role involved in each process?
⬜ Have we identified the major bottlenecks and inefficiencies?
⬜ Do we understand the current technology ecosystem?
⬜ Have we documented all manual workarounds?
⬜ Do we know what information the client struggles to access?
⬜ Have we defined measurable success metrics (KPIs)?
⬜ Do we understand technical, regulatory, and organizational constraints?
⬜ Have we identified required integrations and dependencies?
⬜ Do we understand what "success" means from the client's perspective?
⬜ Can we explain the client's business and pain points without mentioning software?

Guiding Principle: At Copper Cortex, software is never the starting point. We first understand the business, then design processes, and only then define the software that best supports those processes.
